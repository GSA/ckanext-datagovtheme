This page features datasets from participating State and local governments and non-governmental organizations, such as universities, as well as the Federal Government. Datasets from non-Federal Government participants are not Federal Government data and are not subject to the Data.gov Data Policy. Each non-Federal participant maintains its own data policy. It is important that users understand the data policies of participating entities in order to best utilize these datasets. The category of the data provider is clearly marked by a banner (e.g. "Federal," "State," "University" etc.).
